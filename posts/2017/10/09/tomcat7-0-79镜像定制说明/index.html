二、 使用log4j作为默认日志

  • 拷贝log4j-1.2.11.jar、tomcat-juli-adapters.jar、log4j.properties到%TOMCAT_HOME%/lib/目录
  • 拷贝tomcat-juli.jar到%TOMCAT_HOME%/bin/目录
  • 删除%TOMCAT_HOME%/conf/logging.properties

jar包下载地址见(这是tomcat6):https://tomcat.apache.org/tomcat-6.0-doc/logging.html#Using_Log4j
